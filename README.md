# ILC In Lung Public Wiki

This is a sanitized public export of an LLM-assisted research wiki on innate lymphoid cells (ILCs) in lung biology and pulmonary disease.

The public export preserves reusable synthesis pages, including topics, entities, digests, project notes, audit notes, and citation-routing source notes. It intentionally omits raw PDFs, extracted full text, local absolute paths, and machine ingest manifests.

## Scope

The wiki focuses on ILC2 and ILC3 biology in lung and airway contexts, including allergic airway inflammation, respiratory viral infection, tissue repair, neutrophilic and steroid-resistant asthma, stromal niche regulation, metabolism, neuroimmune signaling, and disease-associated plasticity.

## Evidence Language

Claim-level confidence refers to reusable biological claims, not to file-processing status or PDF availability. Provisional source notes should not be treated as mature evidence unless they have been promoted through focused manual crystallization.

## Not Clinical Guidance

This wiki is a research synthesis aid. It is not a clinical guideline, diagnostic tool, or treatment recommendation.
