## Boundaries

- PD-L1 claims are mouse lung/primary helminth-associated type 2 immunity evidence, with papain support for induction; they are not direct human asthma evidence.
- OX40L/OX40 Treg-feedback claims are mouse IL-33/allergen lung evidence; human scRNA-seq analysis is a comparator, not functional proof.
- The OX40L pathway was framed as bidirectional: it can support Th2/Treg expansion and can also be limited by Gata3high Treg feedback.
