# Severe Asthma Sex-Specific ILC Ingest

## Source

- Severe asthma is characterized by a sex-specific ILC landscape and aberrant airway profile that is suppressed by anti-IL-5/5Ralpha biologics
- Science Translational Medicine 17, eadu4913 (2025)
- DOI: 10.1126/scitranslmed.adu4913

## Integration Summary

- Integrated this source as human severe-asthma clinical cohort evidence, not as direct causal proof of ILC2 or ILC3 disease mechanisms.

## Boundaries

- Blood and induced-sputum compartments were kept separate.
- Sex-stratified interpretation was preserved mainly for blood ILC data because sputum sex-stratified analysis was limited by sample availability.
- Anti-IL-5/5Ralpha effects were framed as suppression of IL-5+/IL-13+ airway ILC cytokine-producing populations, not depletion of all airway ILC subsets.
