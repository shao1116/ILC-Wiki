## Key Findings

- Butyrate, but not acetate or propionate, inhibited IL-13 and IL-5 production by murine ILC2s in the source text.
- Systemic and local butyrate administration ameliorated ILC2-driven AHR and airway inflammation in reported models.
- Butyrate inhibited ILC2 proliferation and reduced cytokine production in human ILC2s.
- The mechanism is framed through HDAC inhibitory activity.

## Claim-Level Confidence

- High confidence: butyrate suppresses ILC2 cytokine output and ILC2-dependent AHR in the reported systems.
- Medium-high confidence: human ILC2 cytokine suppression supports translational relevance, but clinical asthma efficacy is not established here.
- Low confidence: do not generalize all SCFAs as equivalent regulators of ILC2s.
