## Key Findings

- Increased production of and sensitivity to PGD 2 is associated with poor asthma control.
- Levels of airway PGD2 and enzymes involved in PGD2 synthesis are upregulated in the lungs of patients with severe asthma.
- 1,2 Furthermore, PGD 2 receptor–bearing cell populations are expanded within the lung in asthmatic patients.
- Notably, human ILC2s are often characterized by high constitutive expression of DP2, and PGD2 is an activating and chemotactic factor for ILC2s.
- After activation by PGD 2, ILC2s produce large amounts of type 2 cytokines, predominantly IL-13.
