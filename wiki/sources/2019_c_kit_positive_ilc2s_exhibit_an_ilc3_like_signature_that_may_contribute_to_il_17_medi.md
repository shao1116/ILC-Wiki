## Key Findings

- The source identifies a c-Kit+ CCR6+ ILC2 subpopulation with ILC3-like features including RORgammat.
- IL-1beta and IL-23 can promote IL-17 production by c-Kit+ ILC2s, while TGF-beta can promote conversion of c-Kit- ILC2s toward RORgammat/IL23R/CCR6/KIT expression.
- The source supports ILC2-to-ILC3-like plasticity as a real interpretive issue for IL-17-mediated pathology.
- This is not a direct lung asthma paper, but it is highly relevant for avoiding misclassification of IL-17-producing ILC states.

## Claim-Level Confidence
