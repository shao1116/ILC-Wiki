## Evidence Profile

- Overall confidence: high for CysLT1R expression on lung ILC2s and LTD4-driven type 2 cytokine induction in the reported assays.
- Directness to this project: high for lipid mediator regulation of lung ILC2 function in allergic airway inflammation.
- Evidence type: flow cytometry, purified lung ILC2 stimulation, calcium flux, montelukast inhibition, in vivo leukotriene administration, Alternaria cochallenge.
- Main biological axis: CysLT1R on lung ILC2 -> LTD4/LTC4/LTE4 signaling -> IL-5/IL-13 and eosinophilic airway inflammation.

## Why It Matters Here

This source anchors the lipid mediator branch of ILC2 regulation. It supports cysteinyl leukotriene signaling as a direct activator of lung ILC2 cytokine output and allergic airway inflammation.

## Key Findings

- CysLT1R was expressed on lung ILC2s from naive and Alternaria-challenged mice, including RAG2-deficient and STAT6-deficient contexts.
- LTD4 rapidly induced IL-5 and IL-13 production by purified lung ILC2s in vitro.
- LTD4-induced ILC2 responses were reduced by the CysLT1R antagonist montelukast.
- In vivo leukotriene administration induced ILC2 IL-5 production, and LTD4 potentiated Alternaria-induced eosinophilia, ILC2 accumulation, and ILC2 proliferation.

## Claim-Level Confidence

- High confidence:
  lung ILC2s express functional CysLT1R in the reported mouse models.
- High confidence:
  LTD4 can directly stimulate lung ILC2 type 2 cytokine output and amplify allergic airway inflammation.
- Medium confidence:
  montelukast-sensitive ILC2 regulation is a plausible therapeutic-relevant axis, but human disease translation requires additional evidence.
- Low confidence:
  this source should not be used to claim that CysLT signaling is the dominant ILC2 regulator in all asthma contexts.

## Methods and Context

- Wild-type, RAG2-deficient, and STAT6-deficient mouse lung ILC2 analysis.
- In vitro purified ILC2 stimulation with LTD4 and antagonist testing.
- Intranasal leukotriene administration and Alternaria cochallenge.
- Readouts include IL-5, IL-13, IL-4, calcium influx, ILC2 proliferation, and BAL eosinophilia.

## Caveats

- Evidence is strongest in mouse lung and Alternaria-related contexts.
- Leukotriene effects may interact with epithelial alarmins and allergen context.
- Human extrapolation should be paired with human ILC2 lipid mediator studies.

## Contradiction and Supersession

- Contradiction/tension: lipid mediators amplify ILC2 responses, while other regulatory axes such as beta2-adrenergic signaling, PD-1, dopamine, or IL-1beta can restrain them.
- Supersession status: not superseded; remains a high-priority lung ILC2 lipid signaling source.
