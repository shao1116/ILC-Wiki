## Key Findings

- Human ILCs in non-diseased tissues were mapped across multiple anatomical sites using flow and transcriptional analyses.
- The source text highlights that human ILCs are less strictly compartmentalized than mouse ILCs.
- Tissue localization affected ILC distribution in a subset-dependent manner.
- Tissue environment drove transcriptional heterogeneity, especially for ILC1 populations across mucosal and lymphoid sites.
