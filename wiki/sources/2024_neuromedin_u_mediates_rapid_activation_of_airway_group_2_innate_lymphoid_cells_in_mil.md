## Why It Matters Here

This source gives human airway support for the NMU/NMUR1 neuroimmune axis that mouse models had already implicated in ILC2 activation.

## Key Findings

- In mild asthma, sputum ILC2s are activated within 7 hours after allergen challenge.
- The source reports increased NMUR1+ sputum ILC2s after allergen challenge, with many NMUR1+ ILC2s expressing IL-5/IL-13.
- The study tests airway NMU and ex vivo ILC2 responses, supporting NMU/NMUR1 as a rapid human airway ILC2 activation pathway.
