## Key Findings

- Type 2 immunity is framed as a cytokine and cellular circuit involving ILC2s, Th2 cells, myeloid cells, antibodies, and tissue cells.
- Innate type 2 immunity is proposed to monitor microbe-rich epithelial barriers and sterile parenchymal borders, including stromal/adventitial niches.
- ILC2s can be understood as tissue surveillance and repair/regeneration modules rather than only allergy-driving cells.
- Allergic pathology is framed as deviation or dysregulation of local sustaining units, especially during developmental or environmental vulnerability windows.
