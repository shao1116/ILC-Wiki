## Key Findings

- IL-25 and IL-33 induced ILC2 accumulation in mediastinal lymph nodes, lung tissue, and BAL fluid of challenged mice with distinct kinetics and potency.
- IL-33 and CXCL16 directly induced ILC2 migration in vitro, whereas IL-25 and CCL25 did not in the same assay.
- Intranasal IL-33 or CXCL16 was sufficient to recruit transferred ILC2s into lungs in vivo.
- IL-33-induced migration involved ERK1/2, p38, Akt, JNK, and NF-kappaB, while CXCL16-induced migration involved ERK1/2, p38, and Akt.
