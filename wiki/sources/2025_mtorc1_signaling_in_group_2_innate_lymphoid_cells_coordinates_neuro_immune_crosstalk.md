## Key Findings

- The source reports increased mTORC1 activation in lung ILC2s during allergic asthma models.
- Raptor deletion reduces IL-5/IL-13 production by ILC2s and protects mice from allergic inflammation in the reported system.
- Rapamycin suppresses ILC2 activation and ameliorates allergic lung inflammation.
- The paper links mTORC1 to neuroimmune crosstalk, including NMU-related ILC2 activation.

## Claim-Level Confidence
